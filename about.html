Who We Are

Ningbo University of Queensland

Ningbo University of Queensland, founded in 1956, has a history of over 60 years. During these long years, the University of Queensland at Ningbo University of Science and Technology has continuously developed and grown, cultivating tens of thousands of outstanding talents and writing a glorious history.

Our History

  • 1956  -

  • At the beginning of the establishment of Ningbo University of Science and Queensland, there were only a few rudimentary school buildings and a few teachers. However, the school always adheres to the original intention of providing high-quality education for students and continuously strives to develop and grow.

  • 1960  -

  • Ningbo University of Science Queensland has reached an important milestone. This year, the school was successfully upgraded to a full secondary school, and the high school department began recruiting students. This marks an improvement in the educational level of Sunshine Middle School, providing more students with opportunities for further education.

  • 2002  -

  • The school has carried out a comprehensive renovation and expansion project, constructing facilities such as teaching buildings, experimental buildings, and sports halls, providing students with a more comfortable learning environment.

  • 2005  -

  • Ningbo University of Science Queensland has begun implementing quality education reform, focusing on cultivating students' comprehensive qualities and practical abilities. The school has established various clubs and extracurricular activities to encourage students to actively participate and showcase their strengths and interests.

  • 2015  -

  • Ningbo University of Science Queensland has completed the construction of a new campus, providing students with a broader learning and living space. The new campus has advanced teaching and sports facilities, providing strong support for the comprehensive development of students.
